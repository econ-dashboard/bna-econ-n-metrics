Business


In the third quarter of 2020, the number of businesses in Livingston County, MO was 485. This is a change of 0.414% since the prior quarter, when the number was 483.


Source: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics


Note: BLS counts the number of "establishments," as defined as an economic unit that produces goods or services, often tied to a single location/activity within a region. This could serve as a proxy for the number of local businesses.

Unemployment


In 2020, the unemployment rate in Livingston County, MO was 3.80%. This is a change of 52.0% since one year prior, when the rate was 2.50%.


Source: Local Area Unemployment Statistic from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics

Average Wage


In the third quarter of 2020, the average weekly wage in Livingston County, MO was $682. This is a change of -5.41% since the prior quarter, when the wage was $721.


Source: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics
