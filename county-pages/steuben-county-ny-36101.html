Business


In the third quarter of 2020, the number of businesses in Steuben County, NY was 2,128. This is a change of 0.0941% since the prior quarter, when the number was 2,126.


Source: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics


Note: BLS counts the number of "establishments," as defined as an economic unit that produces goods or services, often tied to a single location/activity within a region. This could serve as a proxy for the number of local businesses.

Unemployment


In 2020, the unemployment rate in Steuben County, NY was 8.20%. This is a change of 86.4% since one year prior, when the rate was 4.40%.


Source: Local Area Unemployment Statistic from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics

Average Wage


In the third quarter of 2020, the average weekly wage in Steuben County, NY was $1,073. This is a change of -10.9% since the prior quarter, when the wage was $1,204.


Source: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics
